How It Works
LEGAL. VERIFIED. DELIVERED.
01
Create Account
Register and select your account type. Civilians, government officers, dealers — each tier grants specific access.
02
Verify Identity
Submit required documentation. Our compliance engine verifies credentials and background within 24–72 hours.
03
Browse & Purchase
Access your tier-approved catalog. All transactions are escrowed and held pending FFL transfer confirmation.
